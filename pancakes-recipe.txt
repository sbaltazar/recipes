Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup flour
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
 - 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - salt
 - 1 pinch of cinnamon

Directions:
 1. Mix the eggs and the milk

 2. Then add the flour, oil, vanilla extract and the cinammon. Then mix it
 
 3. Preheat the pan with oil, the pour the mix to make a pancake, wait until de top 
    of the pancake looks cooked and flip it until the two sides are cooked.

 4. Fill the pancake with your favorite ingredient, like milk candy or jelly.
